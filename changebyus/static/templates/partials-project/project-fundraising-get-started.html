How Does It Work?

Change By Us uses a their party service called Stripe to help you manage payments from donors.

You'll be directed to Stripe's website to quickly set up an account.

Do my donors need an account?
No they don't. They simply click on the "Donate" button at the bottom of your page and submit a payment using an accredited debit or credit card.

How do I receive the funds?
Earnings are tranfered to your bank account on a 7 day rolling basis.

Does Stripe charge any fee?
Stripes charges 2.9%+30 cents for every success transaction. Its the lowest we found out there. There are no other hidden costs.
